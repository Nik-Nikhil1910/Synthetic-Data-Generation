**1. Entities (The "Things" We're Tracking):**

*   **Farm:** This represents the overall farm itself. It would likely contain information like the farm's name, location, size, and contact information.
*   **Field:** This represents a specific plot of land on the farm. We'd want to track its size, location, soil type, and what's currently planted (or was planted) there.
*   **Crop:** This represents the type of plant being grown (e.g., corn, wheat, soybeans). We'd store information about the crop variety, planting dates, expected yield, and any special care requirements.
*   **Animal:** This represents an individual animal on the farm (e.g., a cow, a chicken, a pig). We'd track its species, breed, birthdate, health records, and any other relevant information.
*   **Employee:** This represents a person working on the farm. We'd store their name, contact information, job title, and pay rate.
*   **Equipment:** This represents the machinery used on the farm (e.g., tractors, harvesters, plows). We'd track its make, model, purchase date, maintenance schedule, and current condition.
*   **Harvest:** This represents the act of gathering crops. We'd track the date, field, crop, and amount harvested.
*   **Treatment:** This represents a medical treatment given to an animal. We'd track the date, animal, type of treatment, and medication used.

*   **Crop** *is grown in* **Fields:** A crop can be grown in many fields. (Many-to-Many)
*   **Farm** *employs* **Employees:** One farm can have multiple employees. (One-to-Many)
